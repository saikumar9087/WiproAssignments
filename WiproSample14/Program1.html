Welcome to Library Management System


A Library Management System is a software designed to efficiently handle the administrative tasks of a library. It streamlines operations such as cataloging, member management, and circulation of books, making it easier for librarians and patrons alike. This system enhances the user experience and ensures accurate tracking of library resources.
